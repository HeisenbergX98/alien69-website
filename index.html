About Alien 69

Alien 69 is a punk-rock band formed in 2022 in Poland. The group launched themselves from South America to the European music scene—specifically the Polish one—with a distinct identity and energy.
Often described as “punksychedelic,” they meld gritty punk energy with trippy, psychedelic overtones. Their Facebook presence humorously proclaims: “We came straight outta South America Galaxy to Europe Galaxy, Planet Poland. We’ve got nothing to do with the #NewJerseyDrones. Listen now our cosmic music.”
